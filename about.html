about us

Freezone Consult Limited was incorporated in August 2008. We are a consortium of independent practicing firms who have come together to offer multi-disciplinary consultancy services to our clients We pride ourselves in offering international quality consulting services at locally competitive prices and have enjoyed significant growth since we began operations in 2013. Our clients span Multilateral Agencies, Public and Private Sector Organizations. Our Project teams are integrated, client-delivery focused blend of diaspora returnees with Blue Chip multi-national experience and local graduates with diverse backgrounds
